Website

This is the back-end screen where you can edit a website's details.

You can also view the website screenshot before you will approve the website.

You can change the status of the website, from published to unpublished and/or from featured to not featured.

You must be aware that changing the status of one pending website it won't affect that website in any way, first you must approve him and after that you can set him as featured/unpublished.
